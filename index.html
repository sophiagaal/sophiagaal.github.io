About
Hello! I am a first year M.S. Student at the Language & Knowledge Lab at KAIST, advised by Minjoon Seo.
My main research goal is to make techniques that ensure neural models are aligned with human values in order to reduce the substantial risks they might have (also known the the "Alignment Problem").
Specifically, I am interested in (1) enabling LMs to truly follow human instructions [Expert LMs, Branch LMs (On-going)] (2) teaching LMs to express their thought process [CoTEVer, 🌧RAIN (On-going)] (3) ensuring LMs share commonsense knowledge as humans do [SICK] (4) enforcing LMs to generate helpful & factual responses [Self Reward (On-going)].
In addition, I also enjoy thinking of neural architectures that might have better inductive biases than Transformers [SG-MLP].

I am actively looking for (1) a summer internship position at Korea, and (2) an intern who could work with me at KAIST for at least 6 months. If you're interested in working with me, please apply through KAIRI (KAIST AI Research Internship Program).
